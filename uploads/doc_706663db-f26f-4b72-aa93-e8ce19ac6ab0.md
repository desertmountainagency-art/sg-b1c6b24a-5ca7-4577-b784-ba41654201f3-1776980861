## Brand & Style

The design system is rooted in the concept of "Temporal Sanctuary." It aims to bridge the gap between modern technology and ancient wisdom, positioning the AI mentor not as a machine, but as a seasoned guide. The target audience consists of individuals seeking growth, clarity, and a reflective space away from the high-velocity noise of standard digital interfaces.

The chosen style is a blend of **Minimalism** and **Tactile Zen**. It prioritizes heavy whitespace to reduce cognitive load and uses organic textures and soft lighting to create a sense of intimacy. The UI should feel like a quiet library or a sun-drenched stone garden—stable, permanent, and breathable. Every element is designed to feel non-technical, moving away from "app-like" rigidness toward a more editorial and human-centric experience.

## Colors

The palette is derived from natural elements to ground the user in a sense of timelessness. 

- **Primary (Deep Forest Green):** Used for the "Future Self" identity and primary actions. It represents growth and stability.
- **Secondary (Muted Ochre):** Used sparingly for accents, highlights, and moments of insight. It evokes the warmth of parchment or late-afternoon sun.
- **Tertiary (Warm Bone):** The primary background color. It is softer than pure white, reducing eye strain and feeling more like natural material.
- **Neutral (Warm Gray):** Used for secondary text and borders to maintain a low-contrast, harmonious environment.

Backgrounds should primarily use the Tertiary Bone, while interactive elements leverage the Deep Forest Green for high-value engagement.

## Typography

This design system utilizes a dual-font strategy to differentiate between the AI's wisdom and the user's utility.

- **The Mentor's Voice (Noto Serif):** Used for all messages, quotes, and reflections generated by the AI. This typeface conveys authority, history, and calm.
- **The Interface (Manrope):** Used for buttons, input fields, navigation, and user-generated text. It is clean, balanced, and functional, ensuring that the tool itself remains unobtrusive.

Line heights are intentionally generous to promote a slow, meditative reading pace. Large headlines should use the serif font to set a poetic tone for each session.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout philosophy follows a **Fixed Grid** with an emphasis on centered, editorial composition. On mobile and desktop, content is constrained to a central column to mimic the feel of a book or a journal.

- **Rhythm:** A base 8px unit governs all spacing.
- **Whitespace:** Use "Section Margins" (64px+) between major thematic shifts to give the user's mind space to breathe.
- **Margins:** Generous outer margins (32px minimum) ensure the interface never feels crowded against the screen edges.
- **Alignment:** Text-heavy sections should be left-aligned for readability, while hero moments and quotes may be center-aligned to create a "shrine-like" focal point.

## Elevation & Depth

Depth in this design system is achieved through **Tonal Layers** and **Ambient Shadows** rather than stark borders.

- **Surfaces:** Use subtle shifts between the Tertiary background and slightly lighter containers to suggest hierarchy. 
- **Shadows:** Shadows must be extremely diffused and low-opacity (2-5%). They should feel like "natural light" hitting a soft surface. Use a slight Y-offset to suggest the element is resting gently above the page.
- **Depth Color:** Shadows should be tinted with the Primary color (Deep Forest Green) at a very low opacity (e.g., 5%) rather than pure black, maintaining the organic warmth of the palette.

## Shapes

The shape language is defined by **Softness and Organic flow**. 

- **Corners:** Use a consistent 0.5rem (8px) radius for standard components to remove "sharpness" without becoming overly "bubbly."
- **Large Surfaces:** Containers and cards should use a 1rem (16px) radius to feel like smooth river stones.
- **Interaction States:** When hovered or active, shapes should not change their radius, but can subtly scale to reinforce a tactile, physical presence.

## Components

- **Buttons:** Primary buttons use the Deep Forest Green with white or bone text. They should have ample internal padding (16px top/bottom, 32px sides) to feel substantial yet elegant.
- **Chips:** Used for "Reflection Tags." These should have a thin, low-contrast border (Muted Ochre) and no fill, keeping them secondary to the main text.
- **Lists:** List items are separated by generous whitespace rather than divider lines. If a divider is necessary, it should be a very faint, 1px line in the Neutral color.
- **Input Fields:** Inputs should be "Underlined" or "Soft Fill" (Tertiary + 2% darkness) to avoid the boxed-in look of traditional forms. They should feel like a blank line in a journal.
- **Cards:** Cards are the primary container for insights. They use the most diffused ambient shadows and a slightly lighter tint of the Tertiary color to stand out from the background.
- **Reflection Prompts:** A specialized component featuring a large Noto Serif quote followed by a Manrope input area, designed to look like a dedicated page in a diary.
- **Voice Visualizer:** A subtle, organic wave animation (using the Deep Forest Green) that appears when the AI is "thinking," mimicking a slow breath.
